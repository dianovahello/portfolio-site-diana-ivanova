Zenvelopes

Zenvelopes is a personal budgeting app for the faint of heart. Native for iOS and Android, it helps apply Japanese philosophy and mindfulness to personal finance.

The UI phase involved understanding native iOS and Android patterns for mobile devices. I used advanced prototyping to test with iOS and Android users.

Cookshop

Cookshop is a male-targeted recipes app for desktop and mobile. Solving men's needs in the kitchen by showing how to host a dinner like James Bond.

UX research involved assessing two areas: men's recipe needs, and the extent of gender bias in existing recipe resources, to find opportunities for a new product. UI process explored the question What is masculine UI? And created a responsive design for the web.

See project

Wild Bloom

“Wild Bloom” is an ethical tea company with an (Oscar) Wilde side. Their signature line of “nude tea” is sourced in micro-quantities in the wild, then packaged with zero processing or additives.

I created brand guidelines and responsive UI for Wild Bloom's e-commerce platform, from sketches to a high-fidelity prototype with animated interactions. The challenge was to combine branding and usability: express the company values with Victorian flair, house extensive information about their products and initiatives, without sacrificing clarity, accessibility, or pleasure.
