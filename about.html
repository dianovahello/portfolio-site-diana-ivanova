Skills and Tools

Graphic design

I started practicing graphic design in 2016 by styling a print dossier and creating promotional material for a theater project. I liked applying my skills to solve a clear practical problem for clients.

Today I've worked on logos and brand guides, icon sets, UI, print, poster, custom illustrations and more. Freelancing helps me stay curious about design tools while honing my communication skills.

Media Art

My practice of media art has let me collaborate with a wide range of people on a number of international projects: architects, animators, a software engineer, a composer. I create conceptual video and audio objects and immersive installations. This is a great way to practice critical thinking, solve unique problems, and create experiences with the viewer in mind.

Product Design

I am a graduate of CareerFoundry's UI design program with a specialization in Front-end Development. I gained a range of key skills there including User Research and Competitive Analysis, Prototyping and Wireframing, Interaction Design, Usability Testing and more. I learned the basics of web development including html, CSS, and JavaScript.

What I enjoy most about this field is that I can draw on all my experiences ‐ visual, technical, and soft ‐ to create something functional, meaningful, and beautiful.
