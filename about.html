About

My story

Bulgarian-born and Brooklyn-based by way of Paris and Tel Aviv, I am an adventurous big-picture thinker with a passion for working across disciplines, and borders.

My practice of media art has given me the chance to collaborate with a wide range of people: architects, animators, a software engineer, a composer - and address the anxieties and dreams people experience as they interact with technology. I create conceptual video and audio objects, as well as immersive installations.

I started practicing graphic design in 2016, by creating the visual identity for a print dossier and promotional materials for a theater project. I found great satisfaction in applying my skills to solve a clear practical problem for clients. Today I've worked on print, package, poster, custom illustrations, logos and brand guides, icon sets, and UI.

As an experience designer I strive to craft meaningful, functional, and beautiful products. I leverage my education in visual art from Pratt Institute and the University of Paris, my expertise as a graphic designer and freelancer in Paris and Tel Aviv, my skills in collaboration and project management related to my art practice, and skills related to developing language workshops for professional adults in Paris: empathy, problem-solving in real time, and applying design thinking to develop a product that helps people achieve their goals.
